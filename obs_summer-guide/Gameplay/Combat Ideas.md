* Damage is based purely on the rank of the card for both player weapons and enemies
* Rank is also the speed of the attack. A player with a rank 2 weapon attacks faster than an enemy with a rank of 6
# Possibilities when committing a player attack:
* Weapon rank < enemy
	* The player attacks faster than the enemy. Damages the enemy and does weapon rank effect + special effect of weapon
	* Weapon becomes unusable this round.
	* Player may attack again
* Weapon rank > enemy
	* Enemy attacks first. Player gets damaged + effect.
	* Player attacks back. Attacks (likely kills) enemy. Next enemy enters.
	* Next round begins, refreshes weapons
* Weapon rank = enemy
	* Player crits. No damage taken + weapon effect. Bonus effect for criting and enemy dies. No weapon durability loss
	* Next round begins, refreshes weapons
* Player is out of usable weapons
	* Enemy gets free attack
	* Next round begins, refreshes weapons

* Weapons can have passive effect or active one. Using active puts weapon on cooldown and uses a durability. Passive effects occur when attacking or being hit
