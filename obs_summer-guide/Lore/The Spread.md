* Commonly refers to the universe that seats the 4 suits: Diamonds, Hearts, Spades, Clubs
* With the conception of *The Spread* came the birth of powerful beings: the [[Player (Red Joker)]] and [[Black Joker]]. These are thematic twins who held some dominion over the red and black suits respectively
* The [[Black Joker]]'s growing desire for completion, the lack of closure, the feeling of disconnection—juxtaposed with the recklessness and spontaneity of the [[Player (Red Joker)]]—sparked unfounded envy and motivated him to seize more control
* This joker's claw for power and completion successfully turned the Hearts from the player
* The desire for deep fulfillment will prove to be his fatal character flaw
* The game begins anew, the player experiencing a recent rebirth, arming themselves with diamonds, and attempting to free the suits from this tyrannous reign
