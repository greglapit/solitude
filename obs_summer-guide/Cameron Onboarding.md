# Description

Creating a card game dungeon crawler. You draw diamond cards as your weapons to fight enemies and bosses of the heart, club, and spade suits. Each weapon you draw has a unique effect on the enemies. Careful, because each suit has their own tricks up their sleeve.

![[test_game_screen 1.gif]]

You have the ability to sharpen and chip the diamonds on each of your cards. Sharpening the diamond card increases its rank, and chipping does the opposite. Usually, sharper diamond cards do more damage, but damage isn't all you need! There are useful and unique effects from each diamond weapon, so make sure to consider them all.

# Rules

You get one action per turn. This action includes summoning a card (courtesy of red joker), or sharpening/chipping your weapon. Once your action is used, you must attack with one of your weapons.

The enemy rank is usually how much damage and health they have. Once you commit an attack, you and the targeted enemy will fight. Whoever's damage is lower will take the difference in health.
